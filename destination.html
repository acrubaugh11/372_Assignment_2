Overview

Paris is a must see destination for any aspiring travellers. The capital and largest city in france, Paris is known as the "City of Light". Paris is situated on the Seine River giving it a beutiful setting with many historic landmarks.

Must-See Spots

  1. Eiffel Tower
  2. Seine River
  3. Louvre Museum
  4. Arc de Triomphe
  5. Notre-Dame Cathedral of Paris
  6. Musee d'Orsay

Visitor Tips

  • "Travel in the summer, the greenery is much more vibrant!"
  • "Always keep with you a small wallet with change and credit card"
  • "Leave all of your highly valuable items safe behind in your hotel/lodging area"
  • "Bring comfortable walking shoes because there is no better way to experience Paris than on foot!"
